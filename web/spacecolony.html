SpaceColony

Multiplayer 2D colony sim — build, mine, farm, and explore an alien planet

🎮 Play Now GitHub

What is SpaceColony?

Land on an alien planet. Build a base. Mine resources. Research technology. Farm crops. Explore underground caves. SpaceColony is a real-time multiplayer colony sim that runs in your browser — no download required. Every player shares the same persistent world, with procedurally generated terrain, biomes, and cave systems.

Phaser 3 Python FastAPI WebSocket PostgreSQL Redis Procedural Generation JWT Auth

Core Features

⛏

Mining & Resources

Mine iron, copper, crystal, and stone from the alien landscape. Ores have HP and respawn over time. Smelt raw ore into plates at your furnace.

🔨

Crafting & Building

Hand-craft walls, paths, signs, and stairs. Build machines — auto-miners, fabricators, furnaces, and storage chests. Research unlocks new recipes.

🌱

Farming

Plant wheat seeds on farm plots. Crops grow in real time (even while offline). Harvest wheat for future food crafting. Hover to see growth percentage.

🗨

Underground Caves

Craft stairs to descend into procedurally generated cave systems. Find deep iron, deep copper, and rare crystals. Multiple Z-levels to explore.

👥

Multiplayer

See other players in real-time. Chat, claim land, and build together. Persistent world shared across all players. Spatial culling for performance.

🔬

Research Tree

Unlock new buildings, machines, underground access, and farming through a tiered tech tree. Research costs resources and time.

🌐

Procedural World

Infinite terrain generated from noise functions — grasslands, forests, deserts, mountains, and oceans. No two sessions look the same. Procedural pixel-art tiles with no sprite assets.

💙

Health & Survival

HP system with a persistent health bar. Death triggers respawn at the colony spawn point. Character sheet shows all your stats.

Getting Started

1. Visit rpg.humbrol2.com and create an account.
2. Press R to open the research tree. Start with Stone Tools.
3. Mine rocks and trees for stone and wood.
4. Craft walls and paths to build your base.
5. Research Smelting to unlock the furnace and smelt ores into plates.
6. Research Farming to grow wheat on farm plots.
7. Research Underground Access and craft stairs to explore caves for rare ores.
8. Claim land with a Claim Flag to protect your builds.
